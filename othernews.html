S.H.E CENTRE : STEM Hub For Empowerment Of Young Women

Welcome To The STEM Hub!

The STEM Hub for Empowerment of young Women (S.H.E), is a pivotal initiative that strives to empower young girls through girls exposure to tech skills as well as important life skill.

What we have:

  • STEM Skills Training:

    Explore Science, Technology,Engineering, and Mathematics.

  • Vocational Skills:

    Includes Hairdressing ,Fashion Designing, Tie & Die, Basic coding, Basic Computer Literacy, and more.

  • Mentorship and internship:

    Gain real-world insights and hands-on experience.

  • Health and wellness Services:

    Prioritize your physical and mental well-being.

  • Immersive Learning:

    Engage in interactive sessions designed to deepen your skills.
